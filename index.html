Aoki foundation

The AOKI FOUNDATION's primary goal is supporting organizations in the brain science and research areas with a specific focus on regenerative medicine and brain preservation. Our vision is to one day see a world where degenerative brain diseases do not exist and science and technology play a direct role in extending the healthy lives of ourselves and our loved ones. The Fund also supports humanitarian causes dear and near to Producer Steve Aoki such as disaster relief and animal-rights.
